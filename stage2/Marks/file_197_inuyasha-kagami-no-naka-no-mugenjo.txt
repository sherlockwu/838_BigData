<p>Inuyasha</p> and company have finally destroyed <p>Naraku</p>. And each start to go in their separate ways. <p>Sango</p> going to find her younger brother <p>Kohaku</p>; <p>Miroku</p> back to his old home; <p>Inuyasha</p>, <p>Kagome</p> and <p>Shippou</p> go to gather the rest of the <p>Shikon</p> shards. Unfortunately this peace is not to last. Shortly afterwards the full moon rises, and just keeps rising each night bringing with it the promise of danger. <p>Kagura</p> and <p>Kanna</p> go in search of a mirror, where locked inside is the ruler of the eternal night, <p>Kaguya</p>. To set her free they must gather five items and drop them into the five lakes of <n>Fuji</n>. Setting her free means the worst danger for <p>Inuyasha</p> and the others. Who are forced to face this new threat.
