Continuing with their adventure at <n>Greed Island</n>, <p>Gon</p> and <p>Killua</p> train furiously under the guidance of Biscuit for better preparation at attempting the game. With their abilities vastly improving and the threat of player killers menacing <n>Greed Island</n>, <p>Gon</p> and company continue the game despite the dangers that they will face with the game activities and encounters with other players. Based on the manga by <p>Togashi Yoshihiro</p>.
