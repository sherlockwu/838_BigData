<n>Kyou</n> kara <n>Maou</n>! revolves around <p>Yuri Shibuya</p>, your average Japanese teenager. One day, <p>Yuri</p> sees a classmate being harassed by bullies. Thanks to this intervention, his friend is able to escape, but unfortunately <p>Yuri</p> becomes the new target of the bullies in the process and gets his head shoved into a toilet. But instead of water, the toilet contains a swirling portal that sucks him into another world, largely resembling medieval <n>Europe</n>. There, he is told that he will become the next <n>Demon King</n> due to his black hair and black eyes, traits only possessed by the demon's royal lineage.

<p>Yuri</p>'s arrival is met with some skepticism by some of the demons, who view him as unworthy to be their king. However, after <p>Yuri</p> wins a duel by utilizing his magical powers, the demons slowly begin to acknowledge him as their monarch. <p>Yuri</p> must now learn what it takes be a true <p>Demon King</p>, as he tries to keep the peace between demons and humans in this strange new realm.
