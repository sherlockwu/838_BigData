1. Fullmetal <n>Al</n>chemist: The Blind <p>Al</p>chemist (<p>Moumoku</p> no Renkinjutsushi) 
Based on a side story of the Fullmetal <p>Al</p>chemist manga published in the Fullmetal <n>Al</n>chemist Perfect Guidebook (2003). It's about a blind alchemist named <p>Jude</p>, who serves the <p>Humbergang</p> family. He once violated the taboo of <n>Al</n>chemy. 

2. Fullmetal <n>Al</n>chemist: Simple People (Shinpuru na <p>Hitobito</p>) 
A story about <p>Riza</p> (why she let her hair grow longer) and <p>Winry</p> (why she had her ears pierced and how she got her earrings). 

3. Fullmetal <n>Al</n>chemist: The Tale of Teacher 
Based on a side story of the Fullmetal <n>Al</n>chemist manga, the third OVA is about <n>Ed</n>ward and <n>Al</n>phonse's teacher, <p>Izumi Curtis</p>. 
The story is compiled of two parts. The first part shows how <p>Izumi</p> (then 18 years old) survived in <p>Mt. Briggs</p> to become an apprentice of a renowned alchemist. The second part shows how <p>Izumi</p> and <p>Sig</p> met and their love at first sight. 

4. Yet Another Man's <n>Battlefield</n> 
The story centres around <p>Roy</p>, aged 18, in a military boot camp, how he met an Ishbalan recruit named <n>Heathcliffe Arbor</n>, how he became a friendly rival to <p>Hughes</p>.
