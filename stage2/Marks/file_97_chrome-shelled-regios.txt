In a post-apocalyptic world overrun with mutated beasts called <p>Limbeekoon</p> or <p>Filth Monsters</p>, humanity is forced to live in large mobile cities called <n>Regios</n> and learn to use special weapons called <n>Dite</n>, by harnessing the power of <p>Kei</p> to defend themselves. In the <n>Academy City</n> of <p>Zuellni</p>, <p>Layfon Alseif</p> is hoping to start a new life and forget his past. However, his past has caught the attention of <p>Karian</p> Loss, the manipulative Student <p>Council President</p> and <p>Nina Antalk</p>, a Military Arts student and <n>Captain</n> of the 17th Military Arts Platoon, who instantly recognizes his abilities and decides he’s the perfect candidate to join her group. However, with a secret past that won’t leave him alone and unknown powers beyond normal, <p>Layfon</p> just might not take it.
