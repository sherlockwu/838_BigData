<p>Shu</p> is a typical Japanese boy, but has an unbeatable, optimistic and determined attitude. However, when he sees a mysterious girl with strange eyes named <p>Lala-Ru</p> up on a smokestack, he is soon pulled into a strange desert world. <p>Shu</p> soon discovers the true terrors of war, which includes genocide, brutal torture, hunger, thirst, and child exploitation. Now <p>Shu</p> is trying to save <p>Lala-Ru</p>, as well as his hard earned, and often reluctant, new friends from the insane dictator, <p>Hamdo</p>. Whether <p>Shu</p> can possibly accomplish saving those he cares about while still holding up to his values remains to be seen.
