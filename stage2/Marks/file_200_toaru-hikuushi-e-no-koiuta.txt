In order to uncover the "end of the sky," as spoken of in ancient mythology, <p>Kal-el Albus</p> is sent to <n>Isla</n>, an island in the sky. There he attends <n>Cadoques High</n>'s <n>Aerial Division</n>, where he enjoys a carefree life with his schoolmates. That is...until a surprise attack by the air tribe drags <n>Isla</n> into a bloody war.
