<p>Kousuke Ueki</p>, a student of the <n>Hinokuni Junior High School</n>, is picked by a <n>Celestial King Candidate</n>, Koba-sen, to participate in a competition where people battle out to become the <n>Celestial King</n> and only the strongest will prevail. Embodied with the ability to turn garbage into trees, <p>Kousuke</p> will be joining in the battle against other junior high school students in this selection. This sets the premise for the earth-friendly battle where the students will pit their powers against the rest, with a reward of the <n>Blank Talent</n> giving motive for students to win.
