<p>Kurosaki Ichigo</p>, the temporary <p>Shinigami</p>(Death <n>God</n>) for <p>Kuchiki Rukia</p>, discovers his mother's killer as one of the <n>Hollows</n> he has been hunting. He does battle with it in a prideful battle of revenge. This Special is actually a flashback of previous episodes in <n>Bleach</n>. After the ending credits, a short plays showing <p>Shinigami</p> and their <p>Zanpakutos</p> to "<n>Number</n> One" from the <n>Bleach</n> Soundtrack.
