The story takes place on an island, where a "<n>Fractale System</n>" is beginning to collapse. One day, <p>Clain</p> finds an injured girl called <p>Phryne</p> under a cliff. She disappears leaving a pendant. <p>Clain</p> sets out for a journey with the girl-shaped avatar <p>Nessa</p> to look for <p>Phryne</p> and discovers the secret of the <n>Fractale System</n>.
