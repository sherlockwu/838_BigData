<p>Pacifica Casull</p> is the most feared and hated person by the followers of the <p>God Mauser</p>. Known as the <p>Scrapped Princess</p>, she is the poison that will destroy the world. To avoid being killed by the zealots of <p>Mauser</p>, <p>Pacifica</p> and her adoptive brother and sister leave the village of <n>Manhurin</n>. Her brother, <p>Shannon</p>, is an expert with the sword while <p>Racquel</p> is proficient with magic. At every step of the way, however, someone is constantly trying to kill <p>Pacifica</p>, hoping to somehow avert the catastrophe that is supposed to befall the world on her 16th birthday.
