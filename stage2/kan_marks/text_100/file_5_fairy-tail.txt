A superficial but kind-hearted celestial mage, <p>Lucy Heartfilia</p>, joins a famous wizarding guild in i<n>Magnolia</n> Town called <n>Fairy Tail</n>, where the members use their powers to earn rewards in exchange for fulfilling quests. Here she befriends an impetuous but faithful dragon-slayer wizard, <p>Natsu Dragneel</p>, who is scouring the land for his missing father. The two form a bond with fellow mages <p>Erza Scarlet</p> and <n>Gray Fullbuster</n>, and together they tackle daunting challenges, taking on several powerful allies and foes along the way. 

Created by <p>Hiro Mashima</p>, Fairy Tail is an action-packed adventure that follows <p>Lucy</p> and her friends as they learn the value of friendship, forgiveness and family.
