In <p>Karakura Town</p>, there has been a sudden outbreak of unidentifiable spirits called "blanks" (vacant souls) while in the skies of <p>Soul Society</p>, the real world has been reflected. A mysterious female shinigami called <p>Senna</p> has appeared before <p>Ichigo</p> along with a man named <p>Ganryu</p>, leading a group called the "Dark Ones."
