A few years after the adventures of the <p>Chosen Children</p> in the <p>Digital World</p>, a new batch of <p>Chosen Children</p> are summoned to save the <p>Digital World</p>. An evil ruler known as the <p><p>Digimon</p> Kaiser</p>, or <p><p>Digimon</p> Emperor</p>, is forcing the <p>Digital World</p>'s <p>Digimon</p> into enslavement. The new group of <p>Chosen Children</p>, with the help of their <p>Digimon</p>, then begin a journey to stop the evil <p><p>Digimon</p> Emperor</p>.
