After participating in the <p>Johto League</p>, <p>Satoshi</p> decides to begin anew in yet another journey; this time by himself and <p>Pikachu</p>, in the land of <p>Houen</p>. Though he isn't alone for long, as 10-year-old <p>Haruka</p> (whom dislikes Pokemon, but just wants to come along to see the world) travels with him after <p>Pikachu</p> destroys her bicycle. <p>Satoshi</p> also meets up with his old companion, <p>Takeshi</p>, and <p>Haruka</p>'s younger brother, <p>Masato</p>. Together, they travel along in <p>Houen</p>, battling <p>Gym Leaders</p>, entering Contests, and other events on the way to the <p>Houen</p> League.
