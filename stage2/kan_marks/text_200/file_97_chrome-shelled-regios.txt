In a post-apocalyptic world overrun with mutated beasts called <p>Limbeekoon</p> or <p>Filth Monsters</p>, humanity is forced to live in large mobile cities called <p>Regios</p> and learn to use special weapons called <p>Dite</p>, by harnessing the power of <p>Kei</p> to defend themselves. In the <p>Academy City</p> of <p>Zuellni</p>, <p><p>Layfon</p> Alseif</p> is hoping to start a new life and forget his past. However, his past has caught the attention of <p>Karian</p> Loss, the manipulative Student <p>Council President</p> and <p>Nina Antalk</p>, a Military Arts student and <p>Captain</p> of the 17th Military Arts Platoon, who instantly recognizes his abilities and decides he’s the perfect candidate to join her group. However, with a secret past that won’t leave him alone and unknown powers beyond normal, <p>Layfon</p> just might not take it.
