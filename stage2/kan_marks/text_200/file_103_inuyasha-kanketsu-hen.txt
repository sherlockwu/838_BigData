<p>Inuyasha</p>, <p>Kagome</p>, <p>Miroku</p>, <p>Sango</p>, <p>Shippou</p> and their neko-mata friend <p>Kirara</p>, are now in the final leg of their quest to put an end to the elusive demon, <p>Naraku</p> and all of the chaos and evil he has caused, and to ultimately undo the unfortunate karma of the <p>Jewel</p> of <n>Four Souls</n>. Their journey, however, will not be easy as their remaining enemies put out all the stops to make sure that they do not accomplish their goal.

This TV <n>Anime</n> depicts Volumes 36-56 (end) of the <p>Inuyasha</p> manga. The story continues from the last moment seen in <p>Inuyasha</p>'s original anime. <p>Inuyasha</p>, <p>Kagome</p>, <p>Sango</p>, <p>Miroku</p> and <p>Shippou</p> are now in the definitive quest to beat <p>Naraku</p> and the evil he has created and absorbed, they don't lose hope since their future depends on it, but the path isn't easy nor short so they will go through life threatening situations and must put their friendship and love on the line with wisdom to know who their friends and foes are to succeed.
