Join <p>Ed</p> and <p>Al</p> as they chase rumors of successful human transmutation into a web of shocking family drama and lies. Sneak a glance at hidden sides of <p>Winry</p> and <p>Hawkeye</p>'s personalities. Survive the frigid north with a young <p><p>Izumi</p> Curtis</p> as she fights to gain a deeper understanding of alchemy. Explore the legendary friendship shared by <p>Mustang</p> and <p>Hughes</p> and watch them grow from military school rivals into hardened brothers transformed by the horrors of the <p>Ishvalan War</p>. (Source: FUNimation) 

1. Fullmetal <p>Al</p>chemist: The Blind <p>Al</p>chemist (<p>Moumoku</p> no Renkinjutsushi) 
Based on a side story of the Fullmetal <p>Al</p>chemist manga published in the Fullmetal <p>Al</p>chemist Perfect Guidebook (2003). It's about a blind alchemist named <p>Jude</p>, who serves the <p>Humbergang</p> family. He once violated the taboo of <p>Al</p>chemy. 

2. Fullmetal <p>Al</p>chemist: Simple People (Shinpuru na <p>Hitobito</p>) 
A story about <p>Riza</p> (why she let her hair grow longer) and <p>Winry</p> (why she had her ears pierced and how she got her earrings). 

3. Fullmetal <p>Al</p>chemist: The Tale of Teacher 
Based on a side story of the Fullmetal <p>Al</p>chemist manga, the third OVA is about <p>Ed</p>ward and <p>Al</p>phonse's teacher, <p><p>Izumi</p> Curtis</p>. 
The story is compiled of two parts. The first part shows how <p>Izumi</p> (then 18 years old) survived in <p>Mt. Briggs</p> to become an apprentice of a renowned alchemist. The second part shows how <p>Izumi</p> and <p>Sig</p> met and their love at first sight. 

4. Yet Another Man's <p>Battlefield</p> 
The story centres around <p>Roy</p>, aged 18, in a military boot camp, how he met an Ishbalan recruit named <p>Heathcliffe Arbor</p>, how he became a friendly rival to <p>Hughes</p>.
