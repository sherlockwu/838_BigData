<p>Kurosaki Ichigo</p>, the temporary <p>Shinigami</p>(Death <p>God</p>) for <p>Kuchiki Rukia</p>, discovers his mother's killer as one of the <p>Hollows</p> he has been hunting. He does battle with it in a prideful battle of revenge. This Special is actually a flashback of previous episodes in <p>Bleach</p>. After the ending credits, a short plays showing <p>Shinigami</p> and their <p>Zanpakutos</p> to "<p>Number</p> One" from the <p>Bleach</p> Soundtrack.
