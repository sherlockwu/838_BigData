After a routine raid, the rookie sky pirate <p>Coud</p> finds a most unusual cargo in his mates' cargo hold: <p>Ren</p>, an "<p>Edel Reid</p>", a race prized by humans for granting special combat power to their partners through "Reacting". He quickly discovers, however, that <p>Ren</p> is even more prized than he expected. The pirate ship is visited by three members of the <p>Edel Reid</p> Complete <p>Protection Agency</p> "<p>Arc Aire</p>", who try to purchase her. When <p>Coud</p> refuses, the ship is suddenly attacked by a mysterious force, and <p>Coud</p>'s captain charges him with <p>Ren</p>'s protection.
