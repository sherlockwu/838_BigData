<p>Simon</p> and <p>Kamina</p> were born and raised in a deep, underground village, hidden from the fabled surface. <p>Kamina</p> is a free-spirited loose cannon bent on making a name for himself, while <p>Simon</p> is a timid young boy with no real aspirations. One day while excavating the earth, <p>Simon</p> stumbles upon a mysterious object that turns out to be the ignition key to an ancient artifact of war, which the duo dubs <p>Lagann</p>. Using their new weapon, <p>Simon</p> and <p>Kamina</p> fend off a surprise attack from the surface with the help of <p>Yoko Littner</p>, a hot-blooded redhead wielding a massive gun who wanders the world above.

In the aftermath of the battle, the sky is now in plain view, prompting <p>Simon</p> and <p>Kamina</p> to set off on a journey alongside <p>Yoko</p> to explore the wastelands of the surface. Soon, they join the fight against the "<p>Beastmen</p>," humanoid creatures that terrorize the remnants of humanity in powerful robots called "Gunmen." Although they face some challenges and setbacks, the trio bravely fights these new enemies alongside other survivors to reclaim the surface, while slowly unraveling a galaxy-sized mystery.
