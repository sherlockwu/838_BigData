The year is 1999. <p>Morioh</p>, a normally quiet and peaceful town, has recently become a hotbed of strange activity. <p><p>Joutarou</p> Kuujou</p>, now a marine biologist, heads to the mysterious town to meet <p><p>Jousuke</p> Higashikata</p>. While the two may seem like strangers at first, <p>Jousuke</p> is actually the illegitimate child of <p>Joutarou</p>'s grandfather, <p>Joseph Joestar</p>. When they meet, <p>Joutarou</p> realizes that he may have more in common with <p>Jousuke</p> than just a blood relation.

Along with the mild-mannered <p>Kouichi Hirose</p> and the boisterous <p>Okuyasu Nijimura</p>, the group dedicates themselves to investigating recent disappearances and other suspicious occurrences within <p>Morioh</p>. Aided by the power of Stands, the four men will encounter danger at every street corner, as it is up to them to unravel the town's secrets, before another occurs.
