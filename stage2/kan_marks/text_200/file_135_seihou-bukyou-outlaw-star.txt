<p><p>Gene</p> Starwind</p> has always dreamed of piloting his own ship out into the vast sea of stars. Unfortunately, not all dreams come true, as he spends his days working odd jobs alongside his partner, <p>James Hawking</p>, on the small planet <p>Sentinel III</p> instead. However, this all takes a turn when the duo takes on a job from <p>Rachel Sweet</p> who, unbeknownst to them, is actually a treasure-hunting outlaw. Tasked with protecting a mysterious girl known as <p>Melfina</p>, the meeting irrevocably changes the pair's lives as they are sent out into the great unknown aboard the highly advanced ship, <p>Outlaw Star</p>.
 
Seihou Bukyou <p>Outlaw Star</p> follows <p>Gene</p> and his ragtag crew as they brave the final frontier, navigating the stars in search of answers to the mysteries surrounding <p>Melfina</p>. Encountering dangerous bounty hunters, space pirates, <p>Taoist</p> mages, and even catgirls, there is sure to be an exhilarating adventure around every corner.
