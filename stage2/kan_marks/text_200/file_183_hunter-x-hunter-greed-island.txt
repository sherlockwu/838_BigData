After the battle with the Spiders, the search for <p>Ging</p> continues as <p>Gon</p> and <p>Killua</p> decided to once again attempt to purchase the rare game "<p>Greed Island</p>". However, <p>Gon</p> came up with a better plan, which was to volunteer to complete the game for the billionaire who owned it. But some training must be done, as <p>Gon</p> and <p>Killua</p>'s abilities are still at a developing stage and "<p>Greed Island</p>" is no simple game for anyone to play with. It is the infamous game that could easily lead to one's death. Based on the manga by <p>Togashi Yoshihiro</p>.
