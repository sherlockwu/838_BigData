<p><p>Makoto</p> Konno</p> is in her last year of high school, but is having a hard time deciding what to do with her future. In between enduring the pressure of her teachers and killing time with her best friends, <p>Makoto</p>'s life suddenly changes when she accidentally discovers that she is capable of literally leaping through time.

<p>Toki</p> wo <p>Kakeru Shoujo</p> follows <p>Makoto</p> as she plays around with her newfound power. However, she soon learns the hard way that every choice has a consequence, and time is a lot more complicated than it may seem.
