Powerful, avaricious sorceress <p>Lina Inverse</p> travels around the world, stealing treasures from bandits who cross her path. Her latest victims, a band of thieves, wait in ambush in a forest, thirsting for revenge. When <p>Lina</p> is about to effortlessly pummel her would-be attackers, the swordsman <p>Gourry Gabriev</p> suddenly announces his presence. Assuming <p>Lina</p> to be a damsel in distress, the foolish yet magnanimous man confronts the brigands in order to rescue her. After defeating them posthaste, the oblivious cavalier decides to escort <p>Lina</p> to <n>Atlas City</n>. Though not very keen on this idea, she ends up accepting his offer.

However, without realizing it, <p>Lina</p> has chanced upon a mighty magical item among her most recent spoils. Now two mysterious men are hunting the young magician and her self-proclaimed guardian to obtain this powerful object for apparently nefarious purposes. This way they begin their adventure, one where the fate of the world itself may be at stake.
