Pokemon are peculiar creatures with a vast array of different abilities and appearances; many people, known as Pokemon trainers, capture and train them, often with the intent of battling others. <p>Young <p>Satoshi</p></p> has not only dreamed of becoming a Pokemon trainer but also a "Pokemon Master," and on the arrival of his 10th birthday, he finally has a chance to make that dream a reality. Unfortunately for him, all three Pokemon available to beginning trainers have already been claimed and only <p>Pikachu</p>, a rebellious <p>Electric</p> type Pokemon, remains. However, this chance encounter would mark the start of a lifelong friendship and an epic adventure!

Setting off on a journey to become the very best, <p>Satoshi</p> and <p>Pikachu</p> travel across beautiful, sprawling regions with their friends <p>Kasumi</p>, a Water type trainer, and <p>Takeshi</p>, a <p>Rock</p> type trainer. But danger lurks around every corner. The infamous Team <p>Rock</p>et is always nearby, seeking to steal powerful Pokemon through nefarious schemes. It'll be up to <p>Satoshi</p> and his friends to thwart their efforts as he also strives to earn the eight Pokemon <p>Gym</p> Badges he'll need to challenge the Pokemon <p>League</p>, and eventually claim the title of Pokemon Master.
