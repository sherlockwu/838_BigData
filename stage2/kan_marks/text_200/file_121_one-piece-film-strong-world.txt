20 years after his escape from <p>Impel Down</p>, the legendary pirate <p>Shiki</p> "the <p>Golden Lion</p>" reappears causing massive upheaval to the Marines. During his long seclusion, he was able to come up with a scheme to bring the <p>World Government</p> to its knees. On his way to execute the plan, <p>Shiki</p> crosses paths with the <p>Straw Hat</p> Pirates and becomes so impressed with <p>Nami</p>'s knowledge of meteorology that he abducts her to forcedly enlist her into his crew. <p>Luffy</p> and the gang end up on a strange land populated with monstrous beasts as they desperately search for <p>Shiki</p> and <p>Nami</p>.
