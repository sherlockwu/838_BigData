With five new kids and an exciting new mission in the <p>Digital World</p>, <p>Digimon Frontier</p> brings back all the great action and adventure of the last three seasons. <p>Takuya</p>, <p>Kouji</p>, <p>Izumi</p>, <p>Junpei</p>. and <p>Tomoki</p> meet each other in a train that takes them to the <p>Digital World</p> where a war against evil is being fought. The <p>Angel</p> digimon, <p>Cherubimon</p>, one of The three angels sent to save the World from the power-hungry <p>Lucemon</p>, has turned to the dark side and the entire <p>Digital World</p> is in peril. To fight this great battle, the five <p>CHOSEN</p> ONES must find the <p>Densetsu</p> no Spirit (Legendary Spirit).
