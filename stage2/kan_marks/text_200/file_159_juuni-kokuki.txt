<p>Nakajima <p>Youko</p></p> is your average somewhat timid high school student. One day, a strange man named <p>Keiki</p> appears before her, swearing allegiance. Before she could properly register what was happening, demon-like creatures attack <p>Youko</p> and her friends, after which they are pulled into a different world. A world unlike what she has ever known. Separated from <p>Keiki</p>, <p>Youko</p> and her friends must do whatever they can if they wish to survive in this new world.
