<p><p>Atsuko</p> Kagari</p> is an ordinary girl who joins the renowned witch academy for witch girls, <p><p>Luna Nova</p> Academy</p>. When she was younger, she went to Magical <p>Festa</p>, a magic show hosted by a witch named <p>Shiny Chariot</p>. <p>Atsuko</p> was so mesmerized and inspired by <p>Shiny Chariot</p>'s performance that she dreamed to someday be a "cool" witch like her. This young and impressionable <p>Atsuko</p> takes <p>Shiny Chariot</p>'s words as her own motto: "Never forget, a believing heart is your magic.“ As part of a class, <p>Atsuko</p> and her classmates go into a labyrinth under the Tower of <p>Luna Nova</p>.
