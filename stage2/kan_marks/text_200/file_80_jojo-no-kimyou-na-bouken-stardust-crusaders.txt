Years after an ancient evil was salvaged from the depths of the sea, <p><p>Joutarou</p> Kuujou</p> sits peacefully within a Japanese jail cell. He's committed no crime yet demands he not be released, believing he's been possessed by an evil spirit capable of harming those around him. Concerned for her son, <p><p>Holly</p> Kuujou</p> asks her father, <p><p>Joseph</p> Joestar</p>, to convince <p>Joutarou</p> to leave the prison. <p>Joseph</p> informs his grandson that the "evil spirit" is in fact something called a "Stand," the physical manifestation of one's fighting spirit which can adopt a variety of deadly forms. After a fiery brawl with <p>Joseph</p>'s friend <p>Mohammed Avdol</p>, <p>Joutarou</p> is forced out of his cell and begins learning how to control the power of his Stand.

However, when a Stand awakens within <p>Holly</p> and threatens to consume her in 50 days, <p>Joutarou</p>, his grandfather, and their allies must seek out and destroy the immortal vampire responsible for her condition. They must travel halfway across the world to <p>Cairo</p>, <p>Egypt</p> and along the way, do battle with ferocious Stand users set on thwarting them. If <p>Joutarou</p> and his allies fail in their mission, humanity is destined for a grim fate.
