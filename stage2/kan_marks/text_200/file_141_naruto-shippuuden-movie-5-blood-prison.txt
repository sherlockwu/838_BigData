After being captured for attempting to assassinate the leader of <p>Kumogakure</p>, the Raikage, and killing <p>Jounin</p> from <p>Kirigakure</p> and <p>Iwagakure</p>, <p>Naruto</p> is imprisoned in <p>Houzukijou</p>, a criminal containment facility also known as the Blood Prison. 

The master of the castle, Mui, uses the ultimate imprisonment jutsu to steal power from the prisoners. In this place, something is aiming for <p>Naruto</p>'s life. The battle to prove his innocence and uncover the truth has begun for <p>Naruto</p> and his friends.
