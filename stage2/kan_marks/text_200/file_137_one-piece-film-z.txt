The <p>Straw Hat</p> Pirates enter the rough seas of the <p>New World</p> in search of the hidden treasures of the <p>Pirate King</p>, Gol D. Roger－One <p>Piece</p>. On their voyage, the pirates come across a terrifying, powerful man, former <p>Marine</p> Admiral <p>Z.</p>

<p>Z.</p>is accused of having stolen the "<p>Dyna Stones</p>", weapons believed to have the power to shake up the <p>New World</p>. The <p>Marine</p> Headquarters believes <p>Z.</p>is about to use it to end the pirate era, and with it, the lives of many innocent people. In fear of such a phenomenal event, marines start to take action against the former admiral.

Even if it means stumbling upon marines and the navy, the <p>Straw Hat</p> Pirates decided to chase after <p>Z.</p>and stop him from causing havoc. As they continue to embark on their ventures, the pirates bump into new and familiar acquaintances.
