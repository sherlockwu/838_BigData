Eleven years after the introduction of internet-connected, augmented reality eyeglasses and visors, <p>Yuuko Okonogi</p> moves with her family to <n>Daikoku City</n>, the technological center of the emerging half-virtual world. <p>Yuuko</p> joins her grandmother's "investigation agency" comprised of children equipped with virtual tools and powerful metatags. She quickly crosses paths with <p>Yuuko Amasawa</p>, an expert hacker of the virtual environment, as <p>Amasawa</p> relentlessly seeks to "unlock" the mystery of a computer virus that emerges from an inaccessible corrupted space.
