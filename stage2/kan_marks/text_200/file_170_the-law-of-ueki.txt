<p><p>Kousuke</p> Ueki</p>, a student of the <p>Hinokuni Junior High School</p>, is picked by a <p><p>Celestial King</p> Candidate</p>, Koba-sen, to participate in a competition where people battle out to become the <p>Celestial King</p> and only the strongest will prevail. Embodied with the ability to turn garbage into trees, <p>Kousuke</p> will be joining in the battle against other junior high school students in this selection. This sets the premise for the earth-friendly battle where the students will pit their powers against the rest, with a reward of the <p>Blank Talent</p> giving motive for students to win.
