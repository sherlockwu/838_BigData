In this world, humans coexist with demonic predators called <p>Yoma</p>. These demonic beasts feast on human innards and can blend into human society by taking on human appearance. As a counter force, a mysterious organization created half-human, half-<n>Yoma</n> warriors known as the "<p>Silver Eyed Witches</p>" or "<p>Claymores</p>," after the huge claymore swords they carry. They are detested by humanity however necessary.

The story begins with a young boy, <p>Raki</p>, who has lost everything in a <p>Yoma</p> attack, and the <p>Claymore</p>, <p>Clare</p>, who is generally detested by society. Throughout the series, <p>Clare</p> and <p>Raki</p> show their deeper qualities, powers and <p>Clare</p>'s never ending devotion to the goal she swore to fulfill in her childhood.
