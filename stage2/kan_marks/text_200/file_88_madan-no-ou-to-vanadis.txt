In a fantasy version of <p>Europe</p>, a war between enemy countries is brewing. One of these countries, <p>Zhcted</p>, has its seven regions ruled by <p>War Maidens</p>, known as <p>Vanadis</p>. Equipped with powerful dragon-carved weapons, <p>Eleonora</p> "<p>Elen</p>" <p>Viltaria</p>, one of the <p>Vanadis</p>, launches an invasion against their neighboring rival country of <p>Brune</p>. Eventually, <p><p>Tigre</p>vurmud</p> "<p>Tigre</p>" <p>Vorn</p>, a young archer and an earl for <p>Brune</p>'s region of <p>Alsace</p>, has his entire army decimated at <p>Elen</p>'s hands. In a strange twist of events, <p>Elen</p> spares <p>Tigre</p>, and gives him the order, "Become mine!" What could be the meaning behind this new alliance?

Adapted from the light novel written by <p>Tsukasa Kawaguchi</p>, <p>Madan</p> no Ou to <p>Vanadis</p> is an epic adventure filled with complex war tactics and beautiful women. Trapped in a multinational conflict, <p>Tigre</p> and <p>Elen</p> are swept up in a war filled with dark secrets, conspiracies, and corruption.
