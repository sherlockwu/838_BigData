A <p>Shinigami</p> named <p>Baishin</p> who was sealed by <p>Soul Society</p> long ago is set free on <p>Earth</p>. On a fateful day <p>Ichigo</p> encounters him and has roughly half of his reiatsu drained. Its now up to <p>Ichigo</p> and the <p>Soul Society</p> to finally put a stop to <p>Baishin</p> and his destruction.
