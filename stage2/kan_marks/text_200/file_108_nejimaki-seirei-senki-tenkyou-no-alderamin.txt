The world is thrown into chaos when the <p>Katjvarna</p> empire takes arms against the <p>Republic</p> of <p>Kioka</p>. Our hero, Ikta, detests war but ultimately has no choice but to become a High <p>Grade Military Officer</p> to defend his land. No one could have ever imagined that this lazy womanizer would eventually become the hero everyone needed.
