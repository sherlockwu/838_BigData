<p><p>Yuu</p> Narukami</p> moves to <p>Inaba</p>, a seemingly quiet and ordinary town, where he quickly befriends the clumsy transfer student <p>Yousuke Hanamura</p>, the energetic <p>Chie Satonaka</p>, and the beautiful heiress <p>Yukiko Amagi</p>. Shortly after <p>Yuu</p>'s arrival, a chain of mysterious killings begin to occur on foggy days. At the same time, rumors about a strange television channel—dubbed the "<p>Midnight Channel</p>"—spread like wildfire; when staring into their TV screen at midnight, a person may see their soul mate.

After witnessing the most recent murder victim on the <p>Midnight Channel</p>, <p>Yuu</p> attempts to watch it again, only to realize that he can traverse into the TV and reach another world overrun with "<p>Shadows</p>," evil creatures of the dark. Realizing the link behind the hidden dimension and the murders, <p>Yuu</p> and his friends attempt to crack the cases by exploring the diabolical world of the <p>Midnight Channel</p> using their "<p>Personas</p>," awakened manifestations of their "true selves."
