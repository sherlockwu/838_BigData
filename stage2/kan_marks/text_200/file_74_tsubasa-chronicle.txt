During an excavation at the mysterious ruins in <p>Clow Country</p>, <p>Syaoran</p> discovers his childhood friend <p>Princess <p>Sakura</p></p> appear on the site with wings that disperse into many feathers. As the feather's disappear to different dimensions, so does <p>Sakura</p>'s memory. In attempts to save <p>Sakura</p>'s life and restore her memory, <p>Syaoran</p> travels through to another world to find a solution. There's only one thing left he can do. Travel through to different dimensions to collect <p>Sakura</p>'s feathers. Helping out with the quest is <p>Kurogane</p>, an exiled ninja from <p>Japan Country</p> who wishes to return to his world, the runaway magician, <p>Fay</p>, who desires to jump between each world never to return to his own and the white meat-bun shaped creature, <p>Mokona</p>.
