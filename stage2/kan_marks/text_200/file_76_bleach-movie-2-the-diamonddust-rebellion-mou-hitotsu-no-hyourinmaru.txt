Assigned to protect a royal procession transporting a powerful artifact called the "<n>Ouin</n>," <n>Squad</n> 10 gathers in the human world as <p>Captain Toushirou Hitsugaya</p> and <p>Lieutenant Rangiku Matsumoto</p> observe the area cautiously. However, the caravan is suddenly struck by a group of assailants who wreak havoc on the procession, stealing the <n>Ouin</n> in the process. After a brief clash with one of the attackers, the distraught <p>Hitsugaya</p> pursues the escaping thieves, leaving behind <p>Matsumoto</p> and the disoriented squad. Following the incident, the <p>Seireitei</p> brands <p>Hitsugaya</p> a traitor for abandoning his post and puts <n>Squad</n> 10 on indefinite lockdown.

In the human world, <p>Ichigo Kurosaki</p> is investigating a spiritual abnormality when he stumbles across the injured <p>Captain</p>, but is caught off guard when <p>Hitsugaya</p> suddenly flees. Soon learning of the situation, <p>Ichigo</p>, <p>Rukia Kuchiki</p>, <p>Renji Abarai</p>, and <p>Matsumoto</p> set off to prove <p>Hitsugaya</p>'s innocence and uncover the truth behind the theft of the <n>Ouin</n>. Meanwhile, a ghost from <p>Hitsugaya</p>'s past haunts his thoughts as he chases down the true culprit.
