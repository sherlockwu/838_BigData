Welcome to the <p><p>Gourmet</p> Age</p>, where happiness is measured by what you eat. In an era in which life revolves around fine cuisine, people will go to great lengths to procure special ingredients which have the power to turn a nice meal into a heavenly delight. However, since these delicious additives usually come from rare, powerful creatures, <p>Gourmet</p> Hunters are called upon to gather them.

The International <p>Gourmet</p> Organization has the utmost power in this world, and is responsible for maintaining the balance of the distribution of ingredients worldwide. One day, Chef <p>Komatsu</p> is sent on a mission to locate one of the most renowned <p>Gourmet</p> Hunters in existence, <p>Toriko</p>, and to hire him for a very special job.

However, astounded by <p>Toriko</p>'s skill and unbelievable passion, what started out as a simple mission gradually grows into a deep friendship. Combine the talents of <p>Gourmet</p> Hunter <p>Toriko</p>, who dreams of creating the ultimate Full <p>Course</p> meal, and Chef <p>Komatsu</p>, who aspires to one day become a Master Chef, and the recipe for success may just be discovered.
