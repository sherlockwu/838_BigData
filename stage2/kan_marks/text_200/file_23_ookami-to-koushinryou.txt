<p>Holo</p> is a powerful wolf deity who is celebrated and revered in the small town of <n>Pasloe</n> for blessing the annual harvest. Yet as years go by and the villagers become more self-sufficient, <p>Holo</p>, who stylizes herself as the "<p>Wise Wolf</p> of <n>Yoitsu</n>," has been reduced to a mere folk tale. When a traveling merchant named <p>Kraft Lawrence</p> stops at <n>Pasloe</n>, <p>Holo</p> offers to become his business partner if he eventually takes her to her northern home of <n>Yoitsu</n>. The savvy trader recognizes <p>Holo</p>'s unusual ability to evaluate a person's character and accepts her proposition. Now in the possession of both sharp business skills and a charismatic negotiator, <p>Lawrence</p> inches closer to his goal of opening his own shop. However, as <p>Lawrence</p> travels the countryside with <p>Holo</p> in search of economic opportunities, he begins to realize that his aspirations are slowly morphing into something unexpected.
