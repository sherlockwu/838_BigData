<p>Holo</p> is a powerful wolf deity who is celebrated and revered in the small town of <p>Pasloe</p> for blessing the annual harvest. Yet as years go by and the villagers become more self-sufficient, <p>Holo</p>, who stylizes herself as the "<p>Wise <p>Wolf</p></p> of <p>Yoitsu</p>," has been reduced to a mere folk tale. When a traveling merchant named <p>Kraft <p>Lawrence</p></p> stops at <p>Pasloe</p>, <p>Holo</p> offers to become his business partner if he eventually takes her to her northern home of <p>Yoitsu</p>. The savvy trader recognizes <p>Holo</p>'s unusual ability to evaluate a person's character and accepts her proposition. Now in the possession of both sharp business skills and a charismatic negotiator, <p>Lawrence</p> inches closer to his goal of opening his own shop. However, as <p>Lawrence</p> travels the countryside with <p>Holo</p> in search of economic opportunities, he begins to realize that his aspirations are slowly morphing into something unexpected.

Based on the popular light novel of the same name, <p>Ookami</p> to <p>Koushinryou</p>, also known as Spice and <p>Wolf</p>, fuses the two polar genres of economics and romance to create an enthralling story abundant with elaborate schemes, sharp humor, and witty dialogue. <p>Ookami</p> to <p>Koushinryou</p> is more than just a story of bartering; it turns into a journey of searching for a lost identity in an ever-changing world.
