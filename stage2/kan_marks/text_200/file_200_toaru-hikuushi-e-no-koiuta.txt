In order to uncover the "end of the sky," as spoken of in ancient mythology, <p>Kal-el Albus</p> is sent to <p>Isla</p>, an island in the sky. There he attends <p>Cadoques High</p>'s <p>Aerial Division</p>, where he enjoys a carefree life with his schoolmates. That is...until a surprise attack by the air tribe drags <p>Isla</p> into a bloody war.
