In the nineteenth century, we find <p>Lydia Carlton</p> living in <n>Scotland</n>, making a living as a fairy doctor. She is one of those rare humans who can see and communicate with fairies. But no one believes her. However, <p>Edgar</p> is in need of someone with a vast knowledge of fairy lore, and <p>Lydia</p> is just that person. After rescuing her, he becomes her employer, but there are many secrets and emotions that seem to follow <p>Edgar</p>, who claims to be the <n>Blue Knight</n>'s <n>Earl</n>.
