In the nineteenth century, we find <p><p>Lydia</p> Carlton</p> living in <p>Scotland</p>, making a living as a fairy doctor. She is one of those rare humans who can see and communicate with fairies. But no one believes her. However, <p>Edgar</p> is in need of someone with a vast knowledge of fairy lore, and <p>Lydia</p> is just that person. After rescuing her, he becomes her employer, but there are many secrets and emotions that seem to follow <p>Edgar</p>, who claims to be the <p>Blue Knight</p>'s <p>Earl</p>.
