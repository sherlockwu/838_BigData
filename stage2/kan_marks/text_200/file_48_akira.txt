<p>Japan</p>, 1988. An explosion caused by a young boy with psychic powers tears through the city of <p>Tokyo</p> and ignites the fuse that leads to <p>World War III</p>. In order to prevent any further destruction, he is captured and taken into custody, never to be heard from again. Now, in the year 2019, a restored version of the city known as Neo-<p>Tokyo</p>—an area rife with gang violence and terrorism against the current government—stands in its place. Here, <p><p>Shoutarou</p> Kaneda</p> leads "the Capsules," a group of misfits known for riding large, custom motorcycles and being in constant conflict with their rivals "the Clowns."

During one of these battles, <p>Shoutarou</p>'s best friend <p><p>Tetsuo</p> Shima</p> is caught up in an accident with an esper who finds himself in the streets of <p>Tokyo</p> after escaping confinement from a government institution. Through this encounter, <p>Tetsuo</p> begins to develop his own mysterious abilities, as the government seeks to quarantine this latest psychic in a desperate attempt to prevent him from unleashing the destructive power that could once again bring the city to its knees.
