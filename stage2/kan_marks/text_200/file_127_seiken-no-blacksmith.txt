Once upon a time, there was a tragic war, in which the power of the <p>devil</p>s was involved. After the war, the <p>devil</p>s' power was banned and the world was restored to peace. <p>Cecily Campbell</p> is a knight, who has been looking for a blacksmith to repair her old sword given by her father. One day, she saw a man fighting with a ruffian who used the tabooed <p>devil</p>'s power. The man defeated him with one blow of his weirdly shaped sword. The man turned out to be a blacksmith called <p>Luke</p>. The fateful encounter was the beginning of the adventure.
