After celebrating their victory against <p>Al-Thamen</p>, <p>Aladdin</p> and his friends depart the land of <p>Sindria</p>. With the end of the battle, however, comes the time for each of them to go their separate ways. <p>Hakuryuu</p> and <p>Kougyoku</p> are ordered to go back to their home country, the <p>Kou Empire</p>. Meanwhile <p>Aladdin</p> announces he needs to head for <p>Magnostadt</p>—a mysterious country ruled by magicians—to investigate the mysterious events occurring in this new kingdom and become more proficient in magic. For their part, encouraged by <p>Aladdin</p>'s words, <p>Alibaba</p> and <p>Morgiana</p> also set off in pursuit of their own goals: training and going to her homeland, respectively.
