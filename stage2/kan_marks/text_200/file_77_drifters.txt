<p>Shimazu <p>Toyohisa</p></p>, whilst involved at the Battle of <p>Sekigahara</p>, manages to mortally wound Ii Naomasa, but is critically wounded in the process. As he walks from the field broken and bleeding, <p>Toyohisa</p> finds himself transported to a corridor of doors, where a bespectacled man at a desk waits for him. This man, <p>Murasaki</p>, sends <p>Toyohisa</p> into the nearest door where he wakes up in another world. There, <p>Toyohisa</p> meets other great warriors like him who have been transported as well, to be part of a group known as "Drifters."
