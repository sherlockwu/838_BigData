UC 0079 - After the success of Operation Odessa, more and more Earth Federation soldiers are sent to earth to free it from the hands of the Principality of Zeon. As <n>Zeonic troops</n> retreat to unknown parts of the world, it's <n>up to the</n> Earth Federation <n>Ground Force</n> to find them and exterminate them.
