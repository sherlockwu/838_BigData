In the Year FC 60, much of mankind inhabits <n>space colonies</n> which orbit the Earth. Dominance over the colonies is decided once every four years by a large tournament in which each nation sends a single representative to fight the others with a giant robot called a Gundam. <p>Domon Kashuu</p> is selected to represent Neo-Japan in one of these tournaments, but he fights <n>less to ensure</n> his nation's victory than to find his brother, <n>who has been</n> blamed for the deaths of <n>Domon's parents</n> and the disappearance of a very dangerous weapon, <n>the Dark Gundam</n> or Devil Gundam.
