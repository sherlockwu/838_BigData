<p>Cinque Izumi</p>, <p>Nanami Takatsuki</p>, and Rebecca Anderson must once again embark on a journey to the continent of Flonyard and participate in the friendly <n>war games</n> of the three allied nations: Biscotti Republic, Galette, and Pastilage. Cinque is Biscotti’s hero, who also happens to be the cousin of Galette’s hero Nanami. <p>Rebecca</p> is Pastillage’s hero and a dear friend of Cinque.

Dog Days'' begins in the human world. Rebecca prepares her things for her journey back to Pastilage from Japan. Meanwhile, <p>Cinque</p> and Nanami set out to travel to Biscotti and Galette, respectively, <n>all the way from England</n>, when suddenly, a freakish streak of bad <n>luck—in the</n> form of lightning, of course—sends them off course. They <n>soon find themselves</n> in the great Dragon Forest, protected by a Dragon Priestess named Sharu. The Dragon Priestess informs them that demons threaten to invade the forest, as well as the whole continent of Flonyard!

It looks like a real war is about to begin in Dog Days''. Can these three heroes save the whole continent from these evil beings?
