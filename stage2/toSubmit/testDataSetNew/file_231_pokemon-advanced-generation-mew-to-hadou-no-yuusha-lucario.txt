This is the 8th Movie.

In the town of Lohta, a festival is thrown praising "Aaron the Wave Leader Hero" who, according to the legend passed down through the town's generations, stopped a war centuries ago by using the power of "the Tree of the World's Origin." <p>Satoshi</p> and <p>Pikachu</p> participate in a battle to decide this year's hero and after their victory, <n>they are</n> chosen as the "<n>Wave Leader Hero</n>." A grand dance party is held in the castle to celebrate the victor, but suddenly the phantom Pokemon Mew appears and takes Pikachu. <n>Soon afterwards</n>, the Wave Leader Pokemon <p>"Lucario"</p> is freed from his centuries-long seal and meets Satoshi. Satoshi must solve the various mysteries of where Pikachu is, what <p>Mew</p>'s after, what the secret of the legend of the Wave Leader is and how it ties in with Lucario's hidden past as he makes his way to "<n>the Tree of the World's Origin</n>" with Lucario.
