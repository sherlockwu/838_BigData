<p>Kurapika</p> became a Hunter to take vengeance on the Class-A crime group <n>Phantom Troupe</n> who massacred his clan for their unique eyes. The eyes of the <p>Kurta</p> clan turn scarlet in times of anger or emotional agitation and are considered the most beautiful treasures in the world. <p>Kurapika</p> continues to pursue the <p>Phantom Troupe</p> but an unknown party has stolen his "scarlet eyes". With the help of Gon, <p>Killua</p> and <p>Leorio</p>, <n>Kurapika' s</n> life was saved. However, the Phantom Troupe shows up before them and blocks their way. <n>The true</n> identity of the one with the No.4 <n>spider tattoo</n> is about to be unveiled.
