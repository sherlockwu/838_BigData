Spinoff based on the Nintendo DS RPG game by SEGA, released in Japan July, 2008. 

In a world where humans serve as livestock <n>for the ruling beastman</n>, and where sand fills the roles of water and fire. <p>Kyrie</p> is recruited by the "World Destruction Committee" to <n>assist in</n> putting an end to their world. <p>Kyrie</p> alone holds the power needed to do it.
