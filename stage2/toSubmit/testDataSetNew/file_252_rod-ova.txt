<p>Yomiko Readman</p> is a lovable, near-sighted bibliomaniac working as a substitute teacher at a <n>Japanese</n> high school. Her real identity, however, is that of a secret agent for the <n>British Library Special Operations Division</n>. Her codename: "The Paper." The moniker denotes her supernatural ability to freely manipulate <n>paper</n> into any object she can imagine, including tools and weapons in her fight against the powerful and self-serving IJIN (Great Historical Figure) Army! Along with her partner, the enigmatic "<p>Ms. Deep</p>," <p>Yomiko</p> travels across the world in attempt to solve the mystery behind the reincarnation of historical figures and their attempt to control the world.
