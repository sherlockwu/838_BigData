The anime adaptation of the final Mushishi manga arc. <p>Ginko</p> stumbles upon a young girl in a mountain <n>with branches</n> and leaves growing out of her body. He later realises that she is the lord of that mountain but finds it strange that a <n>human like</n> her was chosen as the lord. He later meets her older brother who has continued to search for her ever since she disappeared while she was accompanying him on a stormy day. The story is about that girl, Kaya, and how she is torn between continuing her duty as a mushi lord and returning back to her loving human family.

(Source: ANN)
