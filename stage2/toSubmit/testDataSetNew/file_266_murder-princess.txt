In the kingdom of Forland, a coup d'etat occurs which leads to the death of the King. In the last moments of his life, the King sends his second child, Princess Alita Forland to escape. Unfortunately, <n>while fleeing</n>, a life and death situation between <p>Alita</p> and the <n>bloodthirsty bounty</n> hunter Falis causes the two of them to <n>switch bodies</n>. Desperate for the safety of her Kingdom, <p>Alita</p> begs Falis to return and protect the Kingdom, offering herself as collateral. After a swift beating on the rebel forces, Falis resumes the title of Princess of Forland, leading to the birth of the most savage princess in their land, the "Murder Princess."
