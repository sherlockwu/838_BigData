<n>The story</n> takes place a little in the future. Guided by one particular song, <n>young elementary</n> school student <p>Suzuhara Sorata</p>, from the Heisei Era, <n>is warped</n> through a time skip to an unfamiliar place that looks much like the towns from the Meiji or Taishou period from his textbooks. In this world, <n>he meets</n> three young ladies and nine young men and joins them <n>on a journey</n> aboard the mysterious Norn ship, <n>a giant globe</n> that floats in midair.
