While touring Alamos Town—home of the Space-Time Tower—with the beautiful Alice, <p>Ash</p> and his friends discover that the town's special garden has been decimated! Baron Alberto, a dashing but arrogant fellow, immediately blames the destruction on Darkrai.

As if it just overheard the accusation, <p>Darkrai</p> appears and confronts our heroes!

Who is Darkrai?
