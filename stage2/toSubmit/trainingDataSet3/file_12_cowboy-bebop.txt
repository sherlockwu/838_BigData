In the year 2071, humanity has colonized several of the planets and moons of the solar system leaving the now uninhabitable surface of planet <n>Earth</n> behind. The <n>Inter Solar System Police</n> attempts to keep peace in the galaxy, aided in part by <n>outlaw</n> bounty hunters, referred to as "Cowboys". The ragtag team aboard the spaceship <p>Bebop</p> are two such individuals.
 
<p>Mellow</p> and carefree <p>Spike Spiegel</p> is balanced by his boisterous, pragmatic partner <p>Jet Black</p> as the pair makes a living chasing bounties and collecting rewards. Thrown off course by the addition of new members that they meet in their travels—Ein, a genetically engineered, highly intelligent <p>Welsh Corgi</p>; femme fatale <p>Faye Valentine</p>, an enigmatic trickster with memory loss; and the strange computer whiz kid <p>Edward Wong</p>—the crew embarks on thrilling adventures that unravel each member's dark and mysterious past little by little. 

Well-balanced with high density action and light-hearted comedy, Cowboy <p>Bebop</p> is a space Western classic and an homage to the smooth and improvised music it is named after.
