A year after escaping <n>Sword Art Online</n>, <p>Kazuto Kirigaya</p> has been settling back into the real world. However, his peace is short-lived as a new incident occurs in a game called <n>Gun Gale Online</n>, where a player by the name of <n>Death Gun</n> appears to be killing people in the real world by shooting them in-game. Approached by officials to assist in investigating the murders, <p>Kazuto</p> assumes his persona of <p>Kirito</p> once again and logs into <n>Gun Gale Online</n>, intent on stopping the killer.

Once inside, <p>Kirito</p> meets <p>Sinon</p>, a highly skilled sniper afflicted by a traumatic past. She is soon dragged in his chase after <n>Death Gun</n>, and together they enter the <n>Bullet</n> of <n>Bullet</n>s, a tournament where their target is sure to appear. <n>Uncertain</n> of <n>Death Gun</n>'s real powers, <p>Kirito</p> and <p>Sinon</p> race to stop him before he has the chance to claim another life. Not everything goes smoothly, however, as scars from the past impede their progress. In a high-stakes game where the next victim could easily be one of them, <p>Kirito</p> puts his life on the line in the virtual world once more.
