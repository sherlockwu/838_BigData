A few years after the adventures of the <p>Chosen Children</p> in the <n>Digital World</n>, a new batch of <p>Chosen Children</p> are summoned to save the <n>Digital World</n>. An evil ruler known as the <p>Digimon Kaiser</p>, or <p>Digimon Emperor</p>, is forcing the <n>Digital World</n>'s <p>Digimon</p> into enslavement. The new group of <p>Chosen Children</p>, with the help of their <p>Digimon</p>, then begin a journey to stop the evil <p>Digimon Emperor</p>.
