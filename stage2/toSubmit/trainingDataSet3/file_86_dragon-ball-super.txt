Set just after the events of the <n>Buu Saga</n> of <n>Dragon Ball</n> Z, a deadly threat awakens once more. People lived in peace without knowing who the true heroes were during the devastating battle against <p>Majin Buu</p>. The powerful <n>Dragon Ball</n>s have prevented any permanent damage, and our heroes also continue to live a normal life. In the far reaches of the universe, however, a powerful being awakens early from his slumber, curious about a prophecy of his defeat.

Join <p>Gokuu</p>, <p>Piccolo</p>, <p>Vegeta</p>, <p>Gohan</p>, and the rest of the <n>Dragon Ball</n> crew as they tackle the strongest opponent they have ever faced. <p>Beerus</p>, the god of destruction, now sets his curious sights on <n>Earth</n>. Will the heroes save the day and prevent earth's destruction? Or will the whims of a bored god prove too powerful for the <p>Saiyans</p>? <p>Gokuu</p> faces impossible odds once more and fights for the safety of his loved ones and the planet.
