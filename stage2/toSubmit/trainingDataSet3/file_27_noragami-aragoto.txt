<p>Yato</p> and <p>Yukine</p> have finally mended their relationship as god and <p>Regalia</p>, and everyone has returned to their daily life. <p>Yato</p> remains a minor and unknown deity who continues taking odd jobs for five yen apiece in the hopes of one day having millions of worshippers and his own grand shrine. <p>Hiyori Iki</p> has yet to have her loose soul fixed by <p>Yato</p>, but she enjoys life and prepares to attend high school nonetheless.

Taking place immediately after the first season, <p>Noragami Aragoto</p> delves into the complicated past between <p>Yato</p> and the god of war <p>Bishamon</p>. The female god holds a mysterious grudge against <p>Yato</p>, which often results in violent clashes between them. It doesn't help that <p>Bishamon</p>'s most trusted and beloved <p>Regalia</p>, <p>Kazuma</p>, appears to be indebted to <p>Yato</p>. When lives are on the line, unraveling these mysteries and others may be the only way to correct past mistakes.
